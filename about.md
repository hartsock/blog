Shawn Hartsock is a Staff Engineer working on SCM infrastructure. He builds
tools that treat humans and AI agents as collaborating peers.

This blog is the canonical home for his technical writing. The source lives in
[git](https://github.com/hartsock/blog); posts are authored in markdown in a
separate content repository and published here with the `gila blog` toolchain.
Selected essays are cross-posted to [Medium @hartsock](https://medium.com/@hartsock).
